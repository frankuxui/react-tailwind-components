---
title: Accordion
slug: accordion
group: components
description: Muestra un diálogo de alerta con un mensaje opcional, y aguardará hasta que el usuario cierre la ventana de diálogo.
---

### Import

```js
import Accordion from 'components/@ui/accordion'
```

### Usage

```jsx
<Accordion>
  <Accordion.Panel>
    <Accordion.Title>
      Accordion title one
    </Accordion.Title>
    <Accordion.Content>
      <p>
        Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua. Ut enim ad minim veniam
      </p>
    </Accordion.Content>
  </Accordion.Panel>
  <Accordion.Panel >
    <Accordion.Title>
      Accordion title twoo
    </Accordion.Title>
    <Accordion.Content>
      <p>
        Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua. Ut enim ad minim veniam
      </p>
    </Accordion.Content>
  </Accordion.Panel>
  <Accordion.Panel>
    <Accordion.Title>
      Accordion title three
    </Accordion.Title>
    <Accordion.Content>
      <p>
        Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua. Ut enim ad minim veniam
      </p>
    </Accordion.Content>
  </Accordion.Panel>
</Accordion>
```
